Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

In most cases, you don't need a referral to see a physiotherapist. However, some insurance plans may require one. We recommend checking with your insurance provider or calling us to verify.

Please bring your insurance card, a valid ID, any relevant medical records or imaging results, and a list of current medications. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the area being treated.

Treatment sessions typically last 45-60 minutes, depending on your specific needs and treatment plan. Your first visit may take longer as we conduct a comprehensive evaluation.
